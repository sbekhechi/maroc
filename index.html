Explore Morocco With an Expert


Dr. Amine Bekhechi, retired professor of French, is leading a 12 day tour of Morocco starting on February 3rd of 2020.


During this trip you will be learning about the Culture, the History and the Cuisine of Morocco as well as experiencing the beauty of the landscape, architecture, and people of this North African treasure.

Itinerary


The discovery of Morocco will start in the city of Casabalanca, and will continue on to Rabat, Fes, Meknes, the desert of Morocco, Marrakech, Essaouira, and then return to Casablanca.
